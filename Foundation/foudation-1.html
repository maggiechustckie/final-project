What Foundation You Should Buy

Foundation is the most important step to anyones foundation routine. It is the base of your whole look. In order to have it work for you, you need to pick the correct type of foundation. If you have dry skin, stick to a moisturizing formula such as the Hourglass Stick Foundation or if you have combo skin, try the CoverGirl Outlast Foundation and if you have oily skin, try a foundation that is oil free such as the Maybelline Super Stay Full Coverage Foundation.
