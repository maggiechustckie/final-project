How To Apply Foundation

First, make sure you have either a brush or a beauty sponge to evenly spread the foundation across your face. Seccond, Apply a pea size amount on the back of your hand, and then from there put a few dots all over your face. Third, begin blending the foundation into your skin in circular motions using either the brush, sponge or your hands. Don't forget to blend the product down to your neck for a flawless finish.
