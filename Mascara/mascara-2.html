How To Apply Mascara

To apply, first make sure you lashes are curled. Seccond, Remove the wand from the mascara tube by twisting back and forth instead of pumping it up and down. Then, Wipe off excess mascara on a tissue or on the lip of the tube. Next, Place the wand against the base of your lashes and wiggle it back and forth a little and fully coat your lashes. Lastly, repeat this step twice so your lashes are fully coated.
