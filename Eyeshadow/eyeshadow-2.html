How To Apply Eyeshadow

There are no rules when it comes to eyeshadow, you can let your creativity inspire you! Here are some standard guidlines to ensure your look comes out flawless! Use matte tones in the crease of your eyelid, with will give your eyes some definition. Use shimmers, or sparkly shades on your eyes and in your inner corner to make your eyes pop! A fun place to incoporate color is on your bottom lashline. Don't forget to blend everything together so that you have no harsh lines!
