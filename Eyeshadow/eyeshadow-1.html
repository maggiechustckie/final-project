What Eyeshadow You Should Buy

If you want to stick to nuetral shades that you can wear to work, school or a formal function; try the Urban Decay Naked Pallete. If you want to play around with bolder shades, for party and avant garde looks; try the BH Cosmetics Take Me To Brazil: Rio Edition Pallete. If you want a combination of both, so that you can incorporate some color into your nuetral looks, try the Morphe x James Charles Pallete.
