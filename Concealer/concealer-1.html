What Concealer You Should Buy

Concealer is used for many differnt things, so in turn you need many differnt concealers. If you are looking to brighten your undereyes, you should use a peachy tone corrector such as the Benefit Boing Concealer. If you are looking to cover blemishes, use a skin tone concealer with a creamy consistency and a long wearing formula such as the Tarte Shape Tape Concealer. When looking to highlight the face, use a shade two shades lighter than your skin tone, and to contour use a shade two shades darker.
