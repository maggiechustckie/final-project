How To Apply Concealer

To apply concealer, either use the tapered part of a beauty sponge, or a small brush that can fit under the eyes and pin-point specific areas. To brighten the under eyes, apply three small dots of concealer under the eyes and blend. Then set your concealer with a yellow under-eye powder. To cover blemishes, dab a small amount of concealer onto the blemish and pat it into the skin, then set with a skin-tone powder to keep in place. To highlight and contour, add the lighter concealer onto the high points of your face, and add the dark shade of concealer to the hollows of your face. Blend the concealer in with a beauty sponge.
