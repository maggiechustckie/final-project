How To Apply Lip Products

First, make sure your lips are hydrated with a lip balm or lip salve. Then, line your lips with a color that matches your lipstick. Make sure to fully line your lips and shade in the corners with the lip liner. Next, fill your lips in with the lipstick, staying inside the lip line. Finally, to get a shiny look, top your lips with some lip gloss. Tip- If you are wearing bold colors, make sure to not get the lip product on your teeth!
